## *trap_area_cost_ratio* (mapinfo)

`syscall 1, 303 ; trap_area_cost_ratio (0 in, 1 out)`

Divides the total cost of all objects currently spawned by the capacity of the room (YS::Cost / YS::Capacity)

|------|------|------------


#### Return Parameters
